SAAS SUBSCRIPTION AGREEMENT

This SaaS Subscription Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of March 1, 2026
("Effective Date") by and between:

CloudFirst Platform Inc., a California corporation ("Provider")
and
Meridian Enterprises LLC, a Texas limited liability company ("Customer")

1. SERVICES AND LICENSE

1.1 Provider grants Customer a non-exclusive, non-transferable right to access and use the
CloudFirst Platform ("Service") during the Subscription Term, subject to the terms herein.

1.2 The Service includes: (a) the core platform as described in Exhibit A; (b) standard
support during business hours (9am-6pm PT, M-F); (c) up to 500 user seats.

2. FEES AND PAYMENT

2.1 Customer shall pay an annual subscription fee of $240,000 ($480 per user per year for
500 seats), payable in advance within 30 days of invoice date.

2.2 Fees increase by 8% annually upon each renewal term.

2.3 Late payments accrue interest at 1.5% per month or the maximum rate permitted by law.

3. SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENT

3.1 Provider commits to 99.5% monthly uptime, measured as total minutes in month minus
downtime minutes, divided by total minutes in month.

3.2 Scheduled maintenance windows (Sundays 2am-6am PT) are excluded from uptime calculations.

3.3 Service credits: For each 0.1% below 99.5% uptime, Customer receives a credit of 5%
of monthly fees, up to a maximum of 30% of monthly fees in any given month.

4.1 Customer retains all rights to Customer Data uploaded to the Service.

4.2 Provider may use aggregated, anonymized data derived from Customer's use of the Service

4.3 Provider shall maintain SOC 2 Type II certification throughout the Subscription Term.

4.4 Upon termination, Provider shall make Customer Data available for export for 30 days.
After 30 days, Provider may delete Customer Data without further obligation.

or derivative works, even if based on Customer feedback or suggestions.

5.2 Customer grants Provider a worldwide, royalty-free license to use any feedback,

6. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY

6.1 IN NO EVENT SHALL EITHER PARTY'S AGGREGATE LIABILITY EXCEED THE FEES PAID BY CUSTOMER
IN THE TWELVE (12) MONTHS PRECEDING THE CLAIM.

6.2 NEITHER PARTY SHALL BE LIABLE FOR ANY INDIRECT, INCIDENTAL, SPECIAL, CONSEQUENTIAL,
OR PUNITIVE DAMAGES.

7. TERM AND RENEWAL

7.1 Initial term: Three (3) years from the Effective Date.

7.2 Auto-renewal: This Agreement automatically renews for successive one (1) year periods
unless either Party provides written notice of non-renewal at least sixty (60) days prior
to the end of the then-current term.

7.3 Early termination: Customer may terminate for convenience with 180 days written notice,
subject to payment of remaining fees for the then-current term.

8.1 Provider shall indemnify Customer against third-party claims alleging that the Service

8.2 Customer shall indemnify Provider against claims arising from Customer's use of the

9.1 This Agreement is governed by California law.

9.2 Any dispute shall first be submitted to mediation. If unresolved within 60 days,
disputes shall be resolved by binding arbitration under AAA Commercial Rules in
